About Us

Welcome to the Divyang Scholarship Portal, a platform dedicated to empowering differently-abled individuals by helping them access educational opportunities through various scholarship schemes.

Our mission is to ensure that no talent is left behind due to financial constraints or lack of awareness. We connect eligible students with the right schemes, providing an easy-to-use interface to apply, upload documents, and track their scholarship status.

This initiative aims to bridge the gap between accessibility and opportunity, promoting inclusive growth and education for all. We are committed to transparency, simplicity, and a user-friendly experience.

Thank you for being a part of this mission. Together, we can create a more inclusive and educated society.
